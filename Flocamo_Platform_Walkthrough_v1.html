The platform map — one spine, many faces

Flocamo is not five products. It is one set of shared microservices consumed by every channel and tenant. The nStore B2B implementation stays exactly as built — it gains CreditIQ scoring and payout computation as additive service calls. CreditIQ Intelligence — the brain every tenant borrows

CreditIQ is a shared microservice, not a screen. It ingests lender policy documents at lender setup, matches applications against real policy (not just filters), writes decision reports a credit officer would sign, answers questions on uploaded documents, and speaks — multi-language voice for eligibility, bureau explanation, functionality help and lender-process walkthroughs. Consumers: direct partners, PaaS tenants, Fynaptic underwriting, and the nStore lead flow (injected, M10).

02.1 The service at a glance CIQ

Inputs
Lead payload · filters · lender policy docs · bureau PDF · income docs · BSA output · underwriting hints
Engines
Doc parsing → policy extraction → match & rank → decision narrative → conversational Q&A → voice (STT/TTS, multi-language)
Outputs
Ranked lender list + fit reasons · decision report · verdict · chat answers · voice sessions · usage metering
Hard eligibility stays in the Rule Engine (CORE). CreditIQ advises, narrates and ranks — it never overrides a rule-engine reject.
SVCPOST /ciq/match · POST /ciq/report · POST /ciq/chat · WS /ciq/voice · events ciq.decision_ready
RULEAdvisory boundary: rule-engine outcome (Priority/White/Gray/Reject) caps what CIQ may recommend
LOGModel version + prompt/config hash + inputs hash on every inference — reproducible decisions
